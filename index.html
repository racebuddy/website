RaceBuddy

Racebuddy is event software for long distance cycling events.

Helping race directors manage their event from start to finish.

Racebuddy is split into four modules, and you pay per rider per module, so you only pay for the features that you use.

APPLY

Invite riders to apply to a race. Collect basic information and also get riders to answer long questions about their motivation and suitability for the race. Applications do not need to be made in one sitting, applicants can save and return. Collect Race fees from applicants direct to your Stripe Account.

REGISTER

The registration module is for gathering documents (e.g. Health Insurance) from riders on the start list, and for ensuring that any physical checks required in person are complete (e.g. bike check & ID checks).

ARRIVE

Volunteers use the arrivals module to record that riders have arrived at a controlpoint. For races that cross multiple time zones, or have staggered starts, this ensures that accurate times are always recorded and the correct days/hours/minutes are written in rider's brevet cards. This data is then available publicly for viewers at home to know who is in the lead at control points.

Third party integration

Racebuddy can integrate with other third parties to provide up to date and accurate information to ensure there is a single source of truth about the progress of your race. See the documentation site for more information
